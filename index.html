    Make a Payment

    Payments can be made through the web portal, mobile app, or at oh.smartchildsupport.com.

    Quick Payment Options:

    • Online/Credit Card: oh.smartchildsupport.com or call 1-888-965-2676
    • By Mail: Ohio Child Support Payment Central, P.O. Box 182372, Columbus, OH 43218-2372
    • Cash at Office: 1512 S. US Highway 68, Suite N100, Urbana, OH 43078 (bring exact change)

    Include your name and SETS or Social Security Number on all payments.

    How to Apply

    Complete the IV-D Application online at jfs.ohio.gov/child-support or visit our office.

  • Family & Children Services

    Report Abuse or Neglect

    Emergency: To report child abuse or neglect, or abuse of an adult age 60+, call (937) 484-1500. After hours: (937) 653-3409 or 911.

    How to Report: Call (937) 484-1500 and request "Children Services Intake"

    Confidentiality: Reports are confidential. No consequences for reports made in good faith.

    Services Available

    • Child Protective Services
    • Adult Protective Services (age 60+)
    • Foster Care
    • Adoption

    Foster Care & Adoption

    Interested in becoming a foster or adoptive parent? Requirements:

    • Age 21+, single or married
    • Must complete licensing, training, and background check
